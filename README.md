# Overview

This project simulates the accounting operations of a Saudi Arabian electronics trading company by following the complete accounting cycle—from business setup and transaction recording to financial reporting. All documents, worksheets, and reports were developed to reflect practical accounting processes commonly used in SMEs operating in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

---

# Business Scenario

**KSA Electronics Trading Co.** is a fictional electronics trading company that purchases products from suppliers and sells them to customers across Saudi Arabia. The company maintains accounting records, manages Accounts Receivable (AR) and Accounts Payable (AP), processes VAT-compliant transactions, and prepares financial statements at the end of each accounting period.

---

# Project Objectives

- Simulate a complete accounting cycle.

# About This Project

This repository was created as a practical accounting portfolio project to demonstrate the complete accounting cycle of a KSA-based electronics trading company. It combines accounting documentation, Excel workbooks, financial reporting, and realistic business scenarios to showcase entry-level accounting skills applicable to accounting and finance roles in Saudi Arabia.
